Building on the Level 1 fundamentals, this course equips IBM sellers and business partners with the skills needed to confidently position Bob as a strategic AI‑assistant that accelerates modernization, improves DevSecOps hygiene, and boosts developer productivity across hybrid environments. Participants will learn how to articulate the broader business impact, guide customers through a proof‑of‑concept, and recommend the next‑step roadmap without diving into low‑level architecture details. The session blends concise theory with hands‑on labs that showcase real‑world scenarios such as Java version upgrades, RPG/COBOL refactoring, and automated security policy enforcement.

Completing this learning plan will count towards earning a badge.
