problem: >+
  While Claims process is a very complex process that touches multiple internal
  and external systems; it’s is a key subprocess that is very time consuming &
  challenging. Evolution of AI has given rise to very sophisticated frauds where
  fraudsters are employing innovative ways to defraud insurance providers.


  Insurance providers face operational inefficiencies and elevated risks in
  their claims validation processes due to manual, repetitive workflows, siloed
  legacy systems, and limited fraud detection capabilities. This leads to long
  processing times, higher operational costs, increased risk exposure, and poor
  customer satisfaction.  



  Even after traditional automation of the claims process, several difficulties
  persist:


  **Fragmented Data Flow:** Integration between diverse legacy and modern
  systems (Cloud based systems, Cloud-native systems, new AI enabled systems
  etc) remains patchy, resulting in data silos and incomplete visibility.


  **Static Automation:** are Rigid Workflows: Static automation cannot handle
  complex decisioning, anomaly detection, or adapt to changing fraud patterns in
  real time.


  **Security Risks:** Digitization without proper access governance increases
  the risk of unauthorized access to sensitive claims and policyholder data.


  **Inefficient Human Intervention:** Despite automation, suspicious claims
  often require manual review, causing delays due to lack of intelligent
  prioritization and assistance.


  **Limited Fraud Detection:** Static rule-based models miss evolving fraud
  tactics, exposing insurers to financial and reputational risks.

solution: >-
  An Agentic AI-augmented, intelligent, and automated claims validation process
  featuring:


  **Smart workflow engines** for real-time claim reviews – ~75- 80% of claims
  are processed with zero human intervention by the smart workflow engine


  **Agentic AI-powered anomaly and fraud detection:** Autonomous AI agents
  handle dynamic tasks such as anomaly detection, fraud flagging, and adaptive
  decisioning; thus significantly reducing Risk Exposure


  **AI-assisted human review for edge cases:** Augmenting the capabilities &
  increasing productivity of Claims Analyst to handle remaining ~20-25% of
  flagged claims.


  **Continuous** learning and process optimization
business-value: |+
  - Faster claims validation and settlement
  - Improved accuracy, risk management, and fraud detection
  - Enhanced customer experiences
  - Reduced operational costs and human errors
  - Scalable and adaptive workflows aligned with modern business demands
