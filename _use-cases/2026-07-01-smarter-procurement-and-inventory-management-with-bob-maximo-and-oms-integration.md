solution: This solution delivers a **First-of-a-Kind (FOAK) AI-powered
  integration** between IBM Maximo and IBM Sterling Order Management using **IBM
  BoB** as the intelligent integration layer. It bridges the gap between
  enterprise asset management and supply chain fulfillment by automating the
  end-to-end procurement and material replenishment process.BoB securely
  connects to Maximo through MCP, retrieves maintenance requests, asset,
  inventory, and demand information, intelligently transforms the data into
  Sterling OMS-compatible formats, and publishes it to Sterling OMS for
  orchestration. Sterling OMS then performs intelligent sourcing, inventory
  allocation, supplier selection, and order fulfillment while providing complete
  end-to-end visibility. This eliminates custom point-to-point integrations and
  significantly reduces implementation effort.By combining Maximo's asset
  management capabilities with Sterling OMS's intelligent fulfillment engine,
  the solution enables automated material replenishment, faster procurement
  cycles, reduced manual effort, improved governance, and real-time tracking
  across both systems. The result is improved maintenance execution, reduced
  asset downtime, and a scalable, reusable integration blueprint for
  asset-intensive industries such as manufacturing, utilities, transportation,
  and logistics.

This demo showcases how IBM Bob accelerates enterprise integration development by using AI to analyze business requirements and automatically generate integration assets.The demonstration starts with a business user describing an integration requirement in natural language — for example, connecting Maximo Enterprise Asset Management with ERP, procurement, OMS, and supplier platforms to automate asset purchasing and fulfillment [workflows. IBM](http://workflows.IBM) Bob analyzes the requirements, understands the integration patterns, and generates the required APIs, connectors, transformation logic, and orchestration workflows. The generated integration services enable seamless communication between enterprise applications while reducing the need for extensive custom coding.The demo highlights how Bob can automatically document integration flows, apply reusable patterns, support testing, and help teams maintain integrations as APIs and business processes [evolve. By](http://evolve.By) leveraging IBM Bob, organizations can reduce integration complexity, accelerate modernization initiatives, minimize technical debt, and allow teams to focus more on business outcomes rather than manual integration development.
