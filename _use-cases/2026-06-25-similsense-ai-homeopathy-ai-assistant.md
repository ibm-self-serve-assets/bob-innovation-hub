domain: Healthcare
problem: Homeopathy consultations are highly personalized and rely heavily on
  practitioner expertise to analyze symptoms, perform repertorization, consult
  Materia Medica, and identify the most appropriate remedy. This manual and
  knowledge-intensive process makes consultations time-consuming, difficult to
  scale, and dependent on individual experience. Similisense AI addresses this
  challenge by augmenting practitioners with Agentic AI to deliver faster, more
  consistent, and scalable clinical decision support while keeping the
  practitioner in control of the final decision.
solution: Similisense AI is an AI-powered Homeopathy Assistant that augments
  practitioners throughout the consultation lifecycle. It conversationally
  captures patient symptoms, performs AI-assisted symptom analysis,
  repertorization, and Materia Medica matching, and generates prescription
  recommendations for practitioner review. Built using IBM Bob, orchestrated
  with IBM watsonx Orchestrate the solution demonstrates how Agentic AI can
  transform a knowledge-intensive healthcare workflow into an intelligent,
  scalable, and enterprise-ready solution while keeping practitioners in control
  of the final clinical decision.
business-value: >+
  - **Reduces consultation time** by automating symptom analysis,
  repertorization, and remedy recommendation.

  - **Augments practitioner expertise** with AI-assisted clinical reasoning
  while keeping the expert in control of final decisions.

  - **Improves consistency** in remedy selection through structured,
  knowledge-driven recommendations.

  - **Expands access to care** by enabling remote and AI-assisted homeopathy
  consultations.

  - **Accelerates practitioner onboarding** by helping less-experienced
  homeopaths make informed decisions.

  - **Preserves and scales expert knowledge** by digitizing homeopathic
  reasoning into reusable AI agents.

  - **Demonstrates a reusable Agentic AI pattern** that can be adapted to other
  knowledge-intensive industries.

  - **Accelerates AI solution development** using IBM Bob, reducing development
  effort and time-to-value.

  - **Showcases the IBM AI stack** by combining IBM Bob, and watsonx Orchestrate
  in a real-world business use case.
