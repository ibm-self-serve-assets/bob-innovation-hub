Carreras Grupo Logistico is modernizing its IBM i development environment by piloting IBM Bob, an AI-powered development tool that enhances productivity and streamlines workflows. By using AI to explain legacy code, generate documentation, and assist with refactoring, the organization is reducing the time required to manage complex applications while maintaining system reliability. This approach enables Carreras to accelerate innovation and serve as a reference for how AI-assisted development can transform enterprise IBM i environments.
