Got fixed form RPG programs with minimal or no comments? You're not alone. In this video, we show how IBM Bob can analyze your legacy RPG2 code and automatically generate clear, concise, and meaningful comments, line by line.  
Watch as Bob reviews the program structure, including variables, input/output sections, data definitions, and business logic, then inserts helpful comments directly into the code. We walk through the full before-and-after so you can see the difference.
