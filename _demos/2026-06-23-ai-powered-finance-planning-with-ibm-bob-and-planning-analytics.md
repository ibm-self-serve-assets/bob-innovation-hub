This demo showcases how IBM Bob, connected to IBM Planning Analytics via Planning Analytics MCP server, enables healthcare finance teams to connect to TM1 server, query cubes, analyse multi-dimensional data, and generate executive-ready insights — all through natural language queries.Using a Healthcare dataset on the TM1 server (7 cubes including Revenue HC, P&L HC, and Employee Expenses HC), the demo follows a real analyst workflow: discovering available data, analysing costs and revenue drivers, diagnosing forecast accuracy gaps, modelling what-if scenarios, and generating a comprehensive executive report.
