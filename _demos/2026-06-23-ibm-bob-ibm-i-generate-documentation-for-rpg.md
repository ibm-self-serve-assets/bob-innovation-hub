What does it take to document a massive legacy ERP application written in RPG 36? About an hour with IBM Bob. In this video, we point Bob at a large, complex application — complete with CL programs, printer files, logical and physical files, DDS panel groups, and extensive RPG code — and ask for comprehensive documentation from scratch.  
We instruct Bob to deliver multiple artifacts: an overall architectural view, business rules diagrams and documentation, application-by-application hierarchical breakdowns, and a high-level executive overview. Bob reads through every program and file, builds a complete understanding of the application, and generates everything into an organized folder structure in the local workspace.
