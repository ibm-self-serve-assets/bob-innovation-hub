This demo shows an AI-powered dynamic pricing and offer optimization system that combines batch-processed historical insights with real-time signals to deliver intelligent, context-aware decisions across industries.

**Batch Intelligence:** Uses historical data (demand patterns, events, user behavior) to set a strong pricing foundation

**Real-Time Decisioning:** Adapts instantly to live signals like demand, availability, and user interactions

**Contextual Application:** Enables dynamic pricing in Retail & Hotels, and smart offers/bundles in Food & Beverage

**Personalization:** Tailors pricing and deals based on user behavior, group size, and preferences

**Transparency:** Clearly communicates why prices or offers are changing
