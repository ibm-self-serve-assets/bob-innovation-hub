Still working with fixed form RPG from decades ago? In this video, we show how IBM Bob converts an entire fixed form RPG program to fully free form — including handling O-specs, which don't even exist in free format.  
The key is giving Bob the right context upfront. We instruct Bob on how to approach the conversion, specifically to create print files for any O-specs encountered in the original code. From there, Bob reads the source, builds the print file, and works through the program line by line converting every fixed format specification to clean, readable free form RPG.
